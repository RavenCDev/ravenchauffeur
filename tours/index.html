Malahide Castle & Howth

A relaxed premium day close to Dublin — combining the charm of Howth’s coastal scenery with the elegance of Malahide Village and its famous Castle & Gardens. Ideal for visitors who want a calm itinerary, great photos, and time to enjoy each stop without rushing.

The tour includes Howth Village and Howth Head, then Malahide Village and Malahide Castle & Gardens. You’ll also have free time in Howth for lunch and coastal views. One additional activity can be arranged based on your interests (for example, a cliff walk or extended guided time).
