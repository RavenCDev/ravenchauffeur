Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information.

1. Introduction

Raven Chauffeur Ireland is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information We Collect

When you contact or book with us, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Pick-up and drop-off locations
  • Flight details (for airport transfers)
  • Booking details and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Confirm and manage bookings
  • Coordinate pick-ups and transfers
  • Respond to enquiries
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage

We store personal data securely and only for as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Information may only be shared when legally required or strictly necessary to provide the service.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
