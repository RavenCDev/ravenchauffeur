Frequently Asked Questions

If you don’t find your answer here, message us and we’ll reply quickly.

How do I book a transfer or tour?
Send your details through the contact form (or WhatsApp): date, pick-up location, destination, number of passengers, and luggage. We confirm availability, timing, and the final quote.
Do you monitor flight delays?
Yes. For airport arrivals we monitor flight status and adjust pick-up timing where possible. Long delays may incur additional waiting charges depending on timing and availability.
How much waiting time is included?
Dublin Airport: 1 hour included from flight arrival time.
Other locations: 15 minutes included.
Additional waiting time may be charged.
Are tolls and parking included?
Tolls and parking are not included in the quoted rate and may be added if used during the journey.
Can I add stops during a transfer?
Yes. Point-to-point pricing covers direct travel from pick-up to drop-off. Extra stops or additional services may incur extra fees — just tell us in advance.
Do you provide meet & greet at the terminal?
Yes, meet & greet is available on request. Share your flight details and we’ll confirm the best arrangement.
Do you offer tours outside Dublin?
Yes. We provide private, tailored tours across Ireland. Tell us your must-see stops and preferred timing, and we’ll suggest a smooth itinerary.
Can you handle early morning or late night transfers?
Yes, subject to availability. Send the date and time and we’ll confirm quickly.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before Dublin pick-ups (outside Dublin: 24 hours + driving time).
No-shows: full transfer rate applies.
Day trips/transfers cancelled within 24 hours: full payment applies.
What happens if my flight is delayed?
We monitor flight delays and adjust pick-up timing when possible. Significant delays may require an hourly supplement depending on the situation.
Is your service private?
Yes. Our transfers and tours are private — no shared rides, no crowded schedules. It’s built around comfort and timing.
